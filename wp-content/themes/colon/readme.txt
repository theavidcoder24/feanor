Colon is an excellent choice for business websites. It is extremely fast and super easy to setup. It is perfectly suitable for Corporate, Portfolio, Agency, Real Estate, Medical, Events, Construction, Restaurant, Blog, Landing pages, Charity Nonprofit websites etc. It has built-in schema markups to help achieve the Google search rankings you’re looking for. = Installation =
	
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Colon in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on 'Install' button to install and 'Activate' button to use.
